About Me

Hi, I'm Jake, and this is the second web page I've ever built (just HTML+CSS). I'm a full stack web developer in training at Northwestern University's Coding Boot Camp. I have precisely two weeks of class under my belt, but I'm really excited to be a part of this journey and know that I'm going to learn a great deal over the course of the next five-and-a-half months.

I was born in Palo Alto, raised in equal parts Pennsylvania and Texas, and moved here from NYC two years ago. I work in Research Administration at Northwestern University, but when I'm not there, studying, or in class, I love to spend my time reading, distance running, and working on photography. I live in Bucktown with my partner, Kathryn, and dog, Harriet.
