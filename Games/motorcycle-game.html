Dad and Dave's Motorcycle Adventures!

To play this game, open up your browser's console (on Chrome it's Command + Option + "I"). This is a way of looking "under the hood" of a web page!

To play, type "h" to honk your horn, "r" to ride to town to get a coffee and a donut, "n" to ride the Natchez Traze, "b" to eat a burrito, or "t" to get a tuneup.
