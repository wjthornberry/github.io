About

Hi, I'm Jake. I'm a full stack web developer in training at Northwestern University's Coding Boot Camp and this website is a work in progres. What you're currently viewing is the second iteration of this web page, now that I have some HTML, CSS, and, most recently, the addition of Bootstrap under my belt – and I'm really excited to be a part of this journey. I'm incredibly interested in:

  • The design of web pages and applications
  • Their usability, as well User Experience (and Interaction) design
  • How amazing it is to simply be alive and in the world of development in this day and age, as there so many challenging and fascinating problems that developers are working on and, some day, will solve.

I currently work in Research Administration in the Department of Chemistry of Northwestern University, but, when I'm not there, studying, or in class, I love acting, reading, distance running, and working on photography. I live in Chicago with my girlfriend, Kathryn, and dog, Harriet.
